About PAF-IAST

A Unique Collaborative Venture with Austrian and Chinese Universities

Empowering the Next Generation

Pak-Austria Fachhochschule: Institute of Applied Sciences and Technology (PAF-IAST) is a premier institution dedicated to bridge the gap between industry and academia.

Every faculty member, trained in Austria and China, is focused on innovation and high-quality human resource development. We believe in the power of international collaboration to shape a better future.
